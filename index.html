Project Overview

Welcome to the Legacy Garden Project. In this cross-curricular activity, you’ll explore how communities honor individuals, study the history of monuments and memory, and design a mosaic stepping stone to commemorate retiring teachers. This is your chance to turn research and creativity into a lasting tribute.

📋 Project Checklist

📋 Legacy Garden Project Student Checklist

📘 Phase 1: Understanding Commemoration

  • Complete Monument Gallery Walk Graphic Organizer
  • Participate in class discussion on “Commemoration vs. Celebration”
  • Read and annotate articles on public memory
  • Respond to 2 Quickwrite prompts using evidence (Worksheet 7)

🏛️ Phase 2: Historical Research

  • Complete research on 3 monuments/named places (Worksheet 5)
  • Respond to reflection prompts about public memory
  • Select a controversial monument case
  • Analyze 2 articles per side of the argument (Worksheet 6)
  • Write a 1–2 paragraph supported argument

👩‍🏫 Phase 3: Honoring a Retiring Teacher

  • Complete the Legacy Profile Organizer (Worksheet 1)
  • Interview a student, teacher, or gather stories
  • Brainstorm values, phrases, and symbols

🎨 Phase 4: Tribute Design

  • Choose stone shape (circle or square)
  • Sketch design with grid (Worksheet 2)
  • Fill in Symbolism Key
  • Plan for text/quote and student signature

🗣️ Phase 5: Artist Statement & Presentation

  • Write and revise Artist Statement (Worksheet 4)
  • Finalize tribute design blueprint
  • Sign and submit tribute design
  • Participate in gallery or presentation
